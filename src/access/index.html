Access Information

There is wheelchair access for most of the areas of the Launch Party, however Bunker does not have step-free access. Please get in contact at security@kingsaffair.co.ukand we may be able to arrange a ramp to mitigate any accessibility issues.

There is an accessible toilet and gender-neutral toilets.

Due to the logistics of the event, there is unfortunately not a hearing loop available. There is not a BSL interpreter currently attending the launch party, however, if you would feel more comfortable with an interpreter at the event, please get in touch with Security and Infrastructure using the email above.

There will be an accessible quiet space in the coffee shop. If you require any support during the event, members of the committee will be visible - please do not hesitate to speak to one of us so we can make your experience as enjoyable and comfortable as possible.
